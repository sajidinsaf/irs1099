# Creating Your Account

## Register

1. Go to [irs1099.visibleai.com/register](https://irs1099.visibleai.com/register)
2. Enter your details:
   - **First name** and **Last name**
   - **Email address** -- this will be your login
   - **Password** -- minimum 8 characters
   - **Phone number** (optional)
3. Click **Create Account**

You'll be logged in immediately and redirected to your dashboard.

## Verify your email

After registering, check your inbox for a verification email. Click the link in the email to verify your account. The link expires in 24 hours.

:::tip
If you don't see the email, check your spam/junk folder. The email comes from noreply@irs1099.com.
:::

## Sign in

Already have an account? Go to [irs1099.visibleai.com/login](https://irs1099.visibleai.com/login) and enter your email and password.

## Forgot your password?

1. Click **Forgot password?** on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your inbox for a reset link
4. Click the link and set a new password

The reset link expires in 1 hour.

## Next step

After creating your account, [set up your business profile](/business-profile) to start filing.
