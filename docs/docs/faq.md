# Frequently Asked Questions

## General

### Who needs to file 1099s electronically?
If you have **10 or more** information returns to file in a calendar year, you must file electronically per IRS regulations. We recommend electronic filing for everyone -- it's faster and more accurate.

### Is this platform affiliated with the IRS?
No. We are an independent, authorized electronic filing platform that submits your forms to the IRS through their official IRIS system.

### Is my data secure?
Yes. All sensitive data (TIN, SSN, EIN) is encrypted with **AES-256-GCM** before storage. Passwords are hashed with **BCrypt**. All communications use HTTPS/TLS. We follow IRS Publication 4557 guidelines for safeguarding taxpayer data.

## Filing

### When are 1099s due?
- **1099-NEC**: January 31 (both to recipients and to the IRS)
- **Most other 1099s**: January 31 to recipients, March 31 to the IRS (electronic)

### Can I file for prior tax years?
Yes. You can select tax years 2020-2025 when creating a submission.

### How many forms can I include in one submission?
There is no practical limit. The IRS allows up to 100MB per transmission, which can hold thousands of forms.

### Can I file corrections?
Yes. If the IRS accepted your original filing and you need to make changes, you can file a correction. (Feature coming soon.)

### What if my submission is rejected?
Review the error details on the submission page. Common fixes include correcting TIN formats, name mismatches, or missing fields. Create a new submission with the corrected data.

## Account

### How do I change my password?
Click **Forgot password?** on the login page and follow the email instructions.

### Can I change my email address?
Contact our support team to update your email address.

### What happens if I forget my Receipt ID?
Your Receipt ID is stored in your account and visible on the submission detail page. You can always find it in your dashboard.

## Payments

### What payment methods do you accept?
We accept credit and debit cards through Stripe (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover).

### Can I get a refund?
Contact our support team within 30 days of payment for refund requests.

### Is my payment information secure?
Yes. All payments are processed by Stripe, a PCI Level 1 certified payment processor. We never see or store your card details.
