# Payments & Billing

## Pricing plans

We offer two pricing options:

### Pay Per Form -- $2.99/form
Best for small businesses with a few forms to file.
- Any 1099 form type
- AI-powered validation
- Email notifications
- Status tracking

### Professional -- $149/year
Best for businesses filing many forms.
- **Up to 500 forms** included
- Bulk CSV/Excel upload
- Priority support
- AI assistant
- Corrections & replacement filing
- Only **$0.30 per form** (90% savings)

## How to pay

1. From your dashboard, click **Billing**
2. Choose your plan:
   - **Pay Per Form**: Enter the number of forms, click **Pay**
   - **Professional**: Click **Subscribe Now**
3. You'll be redirected to **Stripe** (our secure payment processor)
4. Complete payment with credit/debit card
5. You'll be redirected back to your dashboard

## After payment

- **Pay Per Form**: Your payment is recorded and you can submit that many forms
- **Professional**: Your subscription activates immediately with 500 forms included

## Payment security

All payments are processed by **Stripe**, a PCI-compliant payment processor. We never see or store your card details.

## Subscription management

View your active subscription from the **Billing** page:
- Plan type and status
- Forms used / forms included
- Renewal date

## Receipts

Payment confirmations are sent to your registered email. You can also view payment history from the Billing page.
