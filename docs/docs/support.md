# Support & Contact

## Need help?

We're here to help you with your IRS 1099 filings.

## Contact us

- **Email**: support@irs1099filing.com
- **Hours**: Monday - Friday, 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM EST

## Common issues

### I didn't receive my verification email
1. Check your spam/junk folder
2. Make sure you entered the correct email address
3. Try registering again if the email was wrong
4. Contact support if the issue persists

### My submission was rejected
1. Go to the submission detail page
2. Review the error messages from the IRS
3. Common causes: invalid TIN format, name mismatches, missing required fields
4. Create a new submission with corrected data

### I can't find my Receipt ID
Your Receipt ID is displayed on the submission detail page after a successful submission. Go to your dashboard and click on the submission to view it.

### My payment failed
1. Make sure your card details are correct
2. Check that your card has sufficient funds
3. Try a different payment method
4. Contact your bank if the issue persists

## IRS-specific questions

For questions about IRS regulations, filing requirements, or your tax obligations, contact the IRS directly:

- **IRS Help Desk**: 1-866-937-4130 (toll-free)
- **International**: 470-769-5100
- **IRIS webpage**: [www.irs.gov/iris](https://www.irs.gov/iris)

## Report a bug

Found a problem with the platform? Email us at support@irs1099filing.com with:
- What you were trying to do
- What happened instead
- Screenshots if possible
